WHY JOIN THE TRUE SCHOOL OF MUSIC

The True School of Music gets 100s of applicants each month. We asked students to answer questions about their live here at True School, and based on what they had to say, here are the top 12 reasons for them to choose The True School of Music over any other music school!

1. State of the art facility

True School is 15,000 sq ft of the very best faciity and equipment money can buy. Big brand names such as Yamaha, Fender and Roland equip our state of the art space creating a place where you can learn, collaborate and create in a purpose built environment.

2. Experienced International and Local faculty

Our faculty comes with extensive national and international experience having studied professionally for many years and built up extensive career credits. At True School, you will learn from outstanding musicians and educators who will not only teach you your skills but also inspire and mentor you.

3. Involvement of Industry Legends in Curriculum & Artist Development

In addition to world-class faculty we are also supported by some of the biggest names in the Indian music scene. Artists such as Salim Merchant, Zubin Balaporia and Ranjit Barot are core collaborators in the development of our programmes.

4. Real Work Experience through our Incubator programme

We believe in the value of real-world experience for developing lasting careers in the music industry. To support this we have our very own agency that actively seeks real-world projects for our students to earn and learn from.

5. Holistic approach to music education

Music is a collaborative art form. We have created a centre where all the major components of that collaboration are together. At True School you can jam with other instrumentalists, producers, dj’s and sound engineers all under one roof.

6. Weekly Masterclasses with Industry Experts

Along with your structured course, we host weekly Masterclasses with industry experts. These sessions are designed to give you insights into how to make music your career and to inspire you to reach for success. Tips on how to promote yourself online, how to put a band together and how to create the perfect EP, these are just some of the industry insights we share.

7. Internationally curated curriculum

Our curriculum is curated by Dr Joachim Junghanns.. Joachim earned his Doctorate in musical arts from the prestigious Manhattan School of Music in New York, where he worked an active musician and a teacher for many years. He has worked with international partners to create a world-class curriculum aligned with international standards.

8. International Affiliations

We have unrivalled affiliations with international institutions, The Conservatorium van Amsterdam being the most significant.

9. Great Blend of Western, World and Indian Music Education

We are the most comprehensive music school in India blending western, world and Indian music in a purpose built space. This blend creates the opportunity to experience multiple genres mixing jazz, with latin, rock and Hindustani creating the possibility of new sounds and collaborations.

10. We are in the heart of Mumbai – the entertainment hub of India.

Being in the heart of the entertainment industry means we can call upon experts in every field all of which wish to share their knowledge and experience. It also means we can tap into real-world industry experience as the scene is right on our doorstep.

11. Multiple private practice rooms

Practice makes perfect and at True School we not only provide state of the art teaching space, we also provide extensive rehearsal space that our students can book. Each room is fully equipped and we even provide instruments so you just need to bring your creativity and passion!

12. Industry insights.

Due to our extensive links with industry, we are often in the know about what’s new. We host launches from International Brands like Native Instruments, UA etc. at the school itself which makes our students the first ones to get to see what’s new in the industry
