KEYBOARDS FOUNDATION (HOBBY) COURSE FOR BEGINNERS

Everyone at True School's Foundation Course is mad about music! We truly believe that music is the very fabric of our lives and have devoted ourselves to helping students experience music in a fun and engaging way.

JUNIOR's

Aimed at 13 - 18 year olds who have either come through TSM Kids or just have a passion for rock and pop music and are keen to learn to the next level. Sessions are more challenging than TSM Kids and the song arrangements more complex. Complete beginners are catered to as well as more advanced players. Whatever your level, TSM Junior is a great way to learn music you love, play live each week, meet great people, form bands and just enjoy making cool music.

Age Range: 13-18 years

Date: Tuesday: 6 pm - 7:30 pm

Duration: Once a week for 1.5 hours.

Class Breakdown: 45 mins instrument training + 45 minutes band coaching

ADULTS

Aimed at people aged over 18 years who have either come through TSM Junior or have a passion for rock and pop music and are keen to take their playing to the next level. Sessions are more challenging than TSM Junior and the song arrangements more complex. Complete beginners are catered to as well as more advanced players. Whatever your level, TSM Adult is a great way to learn music you love, play live each week, meet great people, form bands and just enjoy making amazing music.

Age Range: 18+ years

Date: Tuesday 8.00 - 9.30 PM | Wednesday 6.00 - 7.30 PM

Duration: 1.5 hours

Class Breakdown: 45 mins instrument training + 45 minutes band coaching

FACULTY

Archana Hegdekar

Hailing from a musical family, music came quite naturally to Archana. She got her first music lessons from her grandfather Mr. Narasimha Kamath at the age of 3. Her father Mr. Dinesh Kamath has also been her mentor all through the years. Her formal education in Hindustani Classical music began at the age of 8, under the guidance of Mrs. Shruti Gokhale, Mrs. Swaroopa Gadgil and Mrs. Vidula Bhagwat . Currently, Archana is learning music under the renowned Vocalist, Padmashree Padmaja Phenany Joglekar.
One of her biggest achievements till date is singing in the presence of the Songstress Lata Mangeshkar ji, at an award function. Apart from that, participating in various competitions, locally as well as on television, winning in few, Judging a few, Teaching Music, recording albums for an orphanage based in Chennai, and mainly immense support from her parents and family has helped her develop and grow as a Musician.
