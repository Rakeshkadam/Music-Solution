Music Theory and Ear Training

Contents
This module concentrates on teaching the fundamentals of music theory. It covers the language of music, the recognition of tones, harmonies and rhythm and how to translate that into a written form. This module will teach you to listen to intervals and recognize the relationship between notes and understand the context of this in terms of songs and music.


Outcomes :

By the end of Level 1 a successful student should be able to: Aurally Identify all intervals.
Read basic notated score in both treble and bass clef.
Develop their knowledge of intervals and how they are used to build triads.
Use all Major and Minor scales.
All lessons in this module from the middle of term 1 should have at least ten minutes of ear training as well as the theory content.

Archana Hegdekar

Hailing from a musical family, music came quite naturally to Archana. She got her first music lessons from her grandfather Mr. Narasimha Kamath at the age of 3. Her father Mr. Dinesh Kamath has also been her mentor all through the years. Her formal education in Hindustani Classical music began at the age of 8, under the guidance of Mrs. Shruti Gokhale, Mrs. Swaroopa Gadgil and Mrs. Vidula Bhagwat . Currently, Archana is learning music under the renowned Vocalist, Padmashree Padmaja Phenany Joglekar.
One of her biggest achievements till date is singing in the presence of the Songstress Lata Mangeshkar ji, at an award function. Apart from that, participating in various competitions, locally as well as on television, winning in few, Judging a few, Teaching Music, recording albums for an orphanage based in Chennai, and mainly immense support from her parents and family has helped her develop and grow as a Musician.
