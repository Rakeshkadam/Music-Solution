PRO DJ CLASSES

PRO DJ CLASSES

The Pro DJ Classes at True School are aimed at creating DJ’s who not only want to spin music in different formats, but also want to cross over into electronic music production. We cover formats that include Vinyl (Turn Tables) , CD’s (CDJ’s) and Digital Music (Traktor, Ableton & Maschine). Our experienced DJ faculty teaching a world class curriculum, ensures that our students have what it takes to make it, in the electronic music world.
In the Pro DJ school courses are taught during the weekday, evenings and weekends and are designed to be full time or fit around either work or additional study commitments.We also have Foundation workshops which are 1hr per week taught over the weekend and evenings. Whatever your skill level and whatever your ambition, we have a course/workshop that suits you.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of Level 1 a successful student should be able to:
1. Display a high level of fluency with the use of a DAW, having a basic ability to edit, record, synthesise, sequence, understand signal flow, mix projects and use sampling.
2. Develop a basic understanding of the physics of recording in order to explain why certain techniques are used over others.
3. Identify broad genre definitions and compose to those genres.
4.Give constructive peer feedback and verbalise their own ideas in order to pitch them to others. A level of competency in computers.
Students will be given the opportunity to learn production techniques that are used by producers like Trent Reznor, Timbaland, Max Martin and RedOne. They will have had the opportunity to produce ten pieces in a range of production styles using various techniques.

DJ 101 (VINYL DJ)

Contents
This module makes the students learn and apply their skills on the traditional setup of 2 Technics turntables and 1 mixer. The in-depth approach of the material covers all aspects of mixing, as well as music theory and the functionality of the oldest DJ console configuration.

KEDAR SAKHARKAR

Hailing from a musical family, music came quite naturally to Archana. She got her first music lessons from her grandfather Mr. Narasimha Kamath at the age of 3. Her father Mr. Dinesh Kamath has also been her mentor all through the years. Her formal education in Hindustani Classical music began at the age of 8, under the guidance of Mrs. Shruti Gokhale, Mrs. Swaroopa Gadgil and Mrs. Vidula Bhagwat . Currently, Archana is learning music under the renowned Vocalist, Padmashree Padmaja Phenany Joglekar.
One of her biggest achievements till date is singing in the presence of the Songstress Lata Mangeshkar ji, at an award function. Apart from that, participating in various competitions, locally as well as on television, winning in few, Judging a few, Teaching Music, recording albums for an orphanage based in Chennai, and mainly immense support from her parents and family has helped her develop and grow as a Musician.


DJ 202 (CD DJ)

Contents
The Pioneer Pro DJ line has been at the forefront of digital DJing for many years. The CD DJ module will give learners a chance to learn & understand the Rekordbox preparation tool along with legendary Pioneer specific features like looping, hot cues & FX on international standard console equipment models like CDJ2000 NXS, CDJ900, DJM2000 NXS & DJM 850.

DJ 303 (TURNTABLISM)

Contents
A module specifically designed for students who'd wish to study the art of scratch DJing. Special emphasis is given on scratching techniques, beat juggling combos, one deck drumming & sampling. This module is designed to enhance the skillsets of the student and bring a degree of versatility, not just during solo performances but also by being part of a band or a live act.
